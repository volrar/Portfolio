FAQ

When can I expect a reponse?

I typically strive to answer emails within 1-2 businesses days, however external circumstances may prevent this at times. Should a considerable delay be expected it will be listed here, and any emails sent during this time will receive an automatic response details the expected delay length.

Are you always accepting work?

I am always will to hear your ideas and proposals! If I am ever not accepting work, be it for travel, vacation, or any other numerous options it will always be posted on the home page. Additionally, any emails sent to me during this time will receive an automated email stating that they may see a delay in response times.

What's your hourly rate?

Pricing will typically be done on a per job basis. All price negotiations will be agreed upon before any work will be conducted. Hourly rates begin at minimum 22$USD/hr and will include a contract detailing the expected hours worked on a per week basis as well as the expected wage per hour of work.

Something came up and I need to cancel my project...

No worries! You may cancel a project at any time, no questions asked. All details will be subject to the job contract signed before any work was performed. Standard policy states that each project will be subject to a cencelation fee proprtional to the amount of time already spent on the project. Should the project be based on hourly work there will be no cancelation fee, however all hours alrady worked on the project will need to be paid out.

What if I'm not happy with the final result?

All projects are final as of completion. If you are unhappy with the delivered product, I am delighted to provide up to 2 free revisions of the final product not including any feeback provided during the creation process. After, if you are still unhappy with the final result subsequent rates may be applied.

Where can I leave a review?

I currently do not have a review board on this website, however you are always welcome to send me an email stating the good and the bad. I can only improve my skills if you tell me what I can do better! If you'd like to support me further you can find me on LinkedIn and endorse my skills. It's completely free and it helps get my name out there!
